wood projects
by Roee Karelitz






Hi, my name is Roee and one of my hobbis is working with wood - building , fixing and making things with this material

Here i want to present you one of my projecets that i did in 2020

In my home there was a high ceiling that was built 30 years ago in order to be a storage space above the ceiling but wasn't finished , i had to do something about it so i decided to build one from wood

How to start:



1. Planning and drawing sketch

I took the musermaent that i need and diceded how it will look like thinking about the weight it needs to hold

2.List of tools and parts

Handel tools:
  • Hammer
  • scraw driver
  • knife
  • brushes
  • muserment tape

Electirc tools:
  • Drill
  • electric screw driver
  • polishing machine
  • Color sprayer

Parts :
  • 8 mm screw
  • screw anchor
  • white paint
  • Transparent varnish
  • tree beams
  • osb board

3.Collecting wood

I wanted to make this project with used material so i want and look for it in carpentry shop , i fond a big osb board that used to be a delivering box and i fond tree beams that used to be a pergola

4.Lets go to work :

First i saw the beams , polished and paint them,
After they were dry i built the structure in the shpe like "H" so it will be strong and hold the weight,
The next thing was working on the osb board sawing , polishing and spreading the varnish so it will look glossy

Know that i had all the parts ready to be installed i did holes in the wall and fit the beams in a balanced line , first the long beams from side to side and than the small ones between them to give strength , above the "H" structure i place the osb board and checked the balance

After that the last thing to do was making a slide door in the front of the wall to be used daily
